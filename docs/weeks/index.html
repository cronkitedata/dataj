Current & Upcoming

Past weeks

Monday April 4
... and we're almost done! : I'll use this week to lay out the rest of the semester, since there aren't many deliverables. But please keep an eye on this for readings.

Monday March 14
Visualization as a reporting tool : Visualizing data is a whole 'nother skill, but we can borrow from the design and statistics world to help make our reporting more efficient. Don't worry about style here -- think about whether you'll be able to "see" a story easier in infographic form than in numbers.

Monday February 28
Regular expressions, tables and snippets : For the week before Spring Break, we'll have a lab for your replication project and I'll demo some methods to make better-looking tables in your work. We'll also delve more into the regular expressions used in `str_detect` and its cousins, as well as RStudio snippets to save typing.

Monday February 14
Verbs in depth part 2: Group, mutate, join : This is our last week with verbs -- once this week is done, you'll have the tools to do most of what reporters do with data!

Monday February 7
Going further with tidyverse verbs : This week we'll get back to working with journalism-related data, and start going deeper into some of the key verbs that you'll use throughout your R journey.

Wednesday February 2
Getting started with R and R Markdown : We'll get a first glance at the R environment and make sure you're ready to start using good data journalism habits.

Monday January 31
Introduction to programming with R : Get your computer set up for working in R for data reporting and start writing self-documenting programs in R Markdown.

Wednesday January 26
Spreadsheet review II : Mastering the idea of grouping with pivot tables

Monday January 24
Spreadsheet review I : The first of two days' training on spreadsheets. Some of it you may already know, but you may have not learned how to apply it to journalism or to follow standard newsroom practices.

Wednesday January 19
Defining data : Data isn't just numbers. We'll learn how to think broadly about data for story.

Wednesday January 12
The journalistic method : Consuming news as a reporter

Monday January 10
Introductions : Agenda and preparation for the first day!
