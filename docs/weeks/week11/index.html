Preparation & learning materials

If you want to follow along in class, you can either use the computers in the lab, or install OpenRefine on your computer – it doesn’t take up much space. Just unzip it and drag it into your applications or anywhere else you want to launch it from. I’m by no means an expert in using this, but it’s invaluable for fixing city names and standardizing company names. (Windows folks: Use the “Windows kit with embedded java”. )
